The F37s Pro - Smart Bundle is a robust, modern treadmill characterized by a predominantly matte black or very dark charcoal gray finish, giving it a sleek yet powerful appearance. Its overall silhouette features a wide, relatively low-to-the-ground running deck that extends into a substantial, vertically rising console tower at the front. The console screen is positioned roughly at eye level for an adult user, while the handlebars are at waist to chest height. The machine is substantial in depth and width, suggesting stability rather than compactness. The primary body color is a non-reflective matte black or dark gray, with the running belt being a slightly duller black. Accent colors include white for branding and text, red for the safety key and its cord, and vibrant, customizable LED strips that glow either blue or purple. The central feature is a 10.1-inch rectangular touchscreen display with slightly rounded corners, set within a matte black bezel at the top of the console, angled slightly upwards for optimal viewing. The screen typically shows a dynamic workout interface featuring a scenic video alongside various workout metrics like speed, time, distance, calories, and heart rate, presented with white text and colored graphs. Below the screen, the main control panel is integrated into the console, featuring a prominent central red circular emergency stop button to which a bright red safety key with a red cord is attached. Flanking this central button are a green rectangular start button and a red rectangular stop button on the left, and two dark gray/black rectangular buttons with white up and down arrows for speed or incline adjustment on the right. Above these buttons, a horizontal strip displays smaller, possibly touch-sensitive, white icons for "MODE," "PROGRAM," and "VOLUME." Two circular cup holders are integrated into the console on either side of this control panel. The handlebars are matte black, extending forward and slightly upward from the console, with a slight ergonomic curve at the ends, and there are also shorter, horizontal grips directly below the screen. No visible controls are on the main handlebars. The "SPORTSTECH" brand name appears in white, all-caps, sans-serif font on the top bezel of the display, on the front-center motor cover of the running deck, and on the side panels of the running deck near the front. Integrated into the wide, flat side rails of the running deck are linear LED strips that run almost the full length, emitting a continuous blue or purple glow. The machine is constructed primarily from high-quality, smooth, matte-finished plastic for the console housing, side rails, and motor cover, with a textured rubber running belt for grip. Internal metal framing provides structural integrity. Connections between parts are clean and seamless, with no visible external cables apart from the safety key cord. The machine appears to be a fixed, non-folding design. When in use, a person always faces the console/display end of the treadmill, running towards the tall front part, with their feet moving on the black running belt. Hands can swing freely or briefly rest on the handlebars or shorter grips, maintaining an upright, natural running or walking posture. It would be incorrect for a person to face away from the screen or run towards the back of the machine.
