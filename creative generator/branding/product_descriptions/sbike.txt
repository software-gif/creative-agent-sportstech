The sBike - Indoor Cycling Bike is a sleek, robust, and modern indoor cycling machine, predominantly finished in matte black with dark gray and vibrant blue and red accents. Its overall form factor is that of a sturdy spin bike, designed for stability and an immersive user experience. The main frame is constructed from a matte black powder-coated metal, featuring an angular, robust central column that supports the handlebars and a prominent 21.5-inch rectangular touchscreen display with slightly rounded corners. This large display is mounted centrally at the front, above the handlebars, on a sturdy arm, and is tilted slightly upwards for optimal viewing from a seated position. The screen's bezel is thin and matte black, and below it is a matte black speaker bar with a perforated grille and a central circular button that glows blue when idle and red when active, featuring a stylized 'S' or lightning bolt logo. The screen typically shows a user interface with "Streaming Functions" like Netflix, Prime Video, Hulu, and YouTube logos, along with "Your workouts" thumbnails, or live workout classes with instructors, outdoor cycling scenes, and real-time metrics such as speed, heart rate, resistance, distance, and time, often including a leaderboard and an "AUTO ADJUST ON" toggle. The multi-grip handlebars are also matte black, offering various hand positions including a central horizontal bar, upward-curving extensions, and downward-curving drop-bar style grips, connecting to a vertical dark gray post with white adjustment markings. This handlebar post, along with the vertical seat post, is adjustable via black plastic knobs, with the handlebar adjustment knob featuring a small red accent. A large, prominent red circular knob, which also functions as an emergency brake when pressed, is located centrally on the main frame below the handlebars for resistance adjustment. The black padded saddle is mounted on a horizontal and vertical dark gray post, both with white adjustment markings, and a black adjustment knob is visible beneath the seat. Behind the seat, a black holder is integrated to store two small black dumbbells. The front of the bike features a covered flywheel with a distinctive, continuously glowing blue LED ring around its central part, encased in matte black plastic. Black crank arms connect to pedals that feature black toe cages and straps, designed to secure the rider's feet. The bike rests on a wide, stable base with leveling feet at each corner, and small transport wheels are visible at the front base. Branding includes "SPORTSTECH" printed vertically in white, sans-serif font on the main frame below the handlebar post. When in use, a person sits on the saddle, facing forward towards the screen, with their feet secured in the pedals and hands gripping the multi-grip handlebars in various comfortable positions, leaning slightly forward with a relatively straight back, interacting with the touch-sensitive screen and adjusting resistance via the red knob.
